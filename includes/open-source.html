An Open-Source Sandbox

We utilize a GitHub Account to manage this website & guide, our server, our modpack, and our texture pack, which means that anybody can join one of our development teams to help make Betaworld even better!

How Do Pull-Requests Work?

While anybody may suggest a change via Pull-Request, only the Global Betaworld Congress has authority to accept or reject these Pull-Requests. In order to accept one, a the Speaker of the House must introduce a Bill that authorizes the Pull-Request. After a successful legislative vote, the server is updated that night at 12am Eastern Time with any changes made.
