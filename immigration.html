Immigration

When you first arrive in Betaworld, you are known as a Visitor. Visitors have some abilities that Citizens do not. Read carefully!

Visitors may use the /warp command to instantly teleport to any Nation's Capitol City. Visitors cannot build. Visitors can only sight-see and spend money in shops.

When You're Ready To Become A Citizen of Betaworld

Type /immigrate, then follow the on-screen instructions.
